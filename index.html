C&C Childcare

C&C childcare is a licensed in home childcare. Our program offers age appropriate curriculum that will help prepare your child for school along with lots of arts and crafts, story time, outside play and lots of fun! We offer a safe and nurturing environment that allows all of our kids to learn by discovery and play in a positive manner.
